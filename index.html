About Us

Google Developer Group - Limbe (GDG Limbe) is a hands-on technical community, we have some interesting conferences, competitions, and many other creative activities. It's a technical user group for people who are interested in Google's developer technology. Are you in Limbe and will love to be in the mist of Android, App Engine and Chrome (Mobile, Cloud and Web) Hackers ? Then here is the place to come. Teach and learn. The group is managed by volunteers, expert in many fields. And we are always happy to accept new volunteers interested to be part of the organizational team. Disclaimer: GDG Limbe is an independent group; our activities and the opinions expressed here should in no way be linked to Google, the corporation.
